SproutSync is a web-app made that focuses on the well being of plants. Some of the pros that it has are: automatic plant monitoring through our light and humidity sensors, it is less time consuming; also has efficient energy through plug-in solar panels in our sensors; scanning of plants and finding out their names and family, instant notifications for infos of the plant (like humidity, carbon dioxide levels) and others. Our Tech Stack: HTML, Tailwind CSS and normal CSS, Javascript, PHP and MySQL, Bootstrap.
